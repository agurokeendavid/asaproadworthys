We’re Committed to Australian Road Safety

Here at ASAP Roadworthys, our focus is and always has been, absolute road safety at all costs, with a team of government approved examiners, we’ll make sure you and your family embark on your travels, safely.

The ASAP Roadworthy’s company founder is a local road user who was inspired to create a company absolutely focussed on road safety after a vehicle struck his wife and left her hospitalised, which turned out in the end to be at the fault of an unsafe vehicle which had major faults that caused the accident. Since that day it has been ASAP’s mission to prevent anyone else being in harms way due to the fault of an unroadworthy vehicle and ensure all vehicles are SAFE for all road users.

We understand your vehicle safety is an urgency, which is why here at ASAP we operate on a “NEED IT ASAP?” Basis, coming to you quickly and at your convenience, home or work, to thoroughly inspect and examine your vehicle for safe road use.

We pride ourselves on being honest and providing a great experience for all our customers, being transparent having the ASAP team guidance behind you every step of the way.
